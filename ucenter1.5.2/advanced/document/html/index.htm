About UCenter

UCenter in Chinese means "Member Center", which is also on behalf of User U on behalf of You, take the meaning of which is the "Member Center", or "You (end user) center". UCenter is a bridge for direct information transfer between various Comsenz products, through the seamless integration of Comsenz UCenter and other Comsenz products (or other third-party applications) to achieve the user's one-step login, personal information, and unified members management.
In other words, UCenter is an information bridge for miltiple applications to share one user database.

This manual is for a certain web application development experience in the reader. The aim is to provide the latest and most complete index of UCenter interface function, facilitate the development of applications based on UCenter.
